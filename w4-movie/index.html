About the movie The Mask of Zorro

In Old California 1821, a black-caped masked avenger named Don Diego de la Vega or "Zorro" finds himself in jail after humiliating the evil Spanish governor Don Rafael Montero. His only daughter being raised by Don Rafael as his own, the swordsman makes a daring escape two decades later, and takes under his wing the outlaw Alejandro Murietta to try to teach him the ropes and become the next "Zorro". The stage is set for a ferocious final confrontation, as the young new swordsman prepares to thwart the despicable governor's plans. Can Alejandro live up to the legendary Zorro's name?
